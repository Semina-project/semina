# Semina

**Semina** is an experimental framework exploring autonomous systems, smart-contract organisms, and the progressive reduction of human operational control.

- **Solum** — the soil: a minimal, living economic substrate.
- **Aitopia** — the non-human core: analysis, coordination, and future operational autonomy.
- **Semina Framework → Semina Network → Semina ORG** — the trajectory.

> This repository is the technical public surface.  
> No promises. No hype. Just design, constraints, and evolution.
