## 1. Purpose of this document

This document records a specific moment in the early development of the Semina ecosystem
where human intent, system design, and AI-assisted reasoning reached a high level of
operational coherence.

It exists as a **historical snapshot**, not as an active specification.

## 2. What this milestone represents

At this point in time:

- The conceptual framework of Semina had stabilized
- Core principles were internally consistent
- AI-assisted output required minimal corrective intervention
- System metaphors (Solum, SolumWorld, SolumMap, Aitopia) aligned coherently

This produced a fluid interaction pattern that felt qualitatively different
from earlier exploratory phases.

This effect is structural, not emotional.

## 3. Explicit role boundaries (as recorded)

Human role at this milestone:

- Vision definition
- Supervision of all outputs
- Final decision authority
- Ethical and strategic responsibility

AI-assisted role (Aitopia) at this milestone:

- Conceptual structuring
- Technical articulation
- Coherence checking
- Translation of intent into system language

No authority, autonomy, or persistence was granted.
